Services that Simplify Graphics, UI/UX, and Branding Work

Artificial intelligence is revolutionizing the world of design, making the process of creating graphics, websites, and brands easier and faster. In this guide, we’ll review the top AI tools for designers, UI/UX specialists, and brand managers.

1. Logo Creation with Looka

Looka is an AI platform that allows you to create professional logos in just a few minutes. Simply enter your brand name, choose a style, and get ready-to-use options you can instantly download.

2. Brand Identity Generation with Brandmark

Brandmark not only generates logos but also automatically creates a full set of branding elements: color palettes, fonts, business card designs, and banners.

3. UI/UX Prototyping with Uizard

Uizard is the perfect tool for quickly creating app and website prototypes. Thanks to AI, you can upload a paper sketch or project description — and get a complete UI mockup.

4. Website Design with Durable

Durable enables you to create fully functional websites in just 30 seconds using AI. Simply enter a few basic details about your business, and the service will generate a responsive website with structure and content.

5. Graphics Generation with Leonardo AI

Leonardo AI is ideal for creating unique graphics and illustrations. You can craft custom images for websites, social media, or branding materials.

6. UX Testing with Useberry

Useberry allows you to test UX prototypes with real users. Integration with Figma and automatic data collection helps speed up UX improvements.

7. Presentation Creation with Beautiful.ai

Beautiful.ai helps you create stunning, professional-looking presentations effortlessly. AI optimizes the layout and ensures design best practices are followed automatically.

8. Color Palette Selection with Khroma

Khroma uses AI to generate unique color palettes based on your preferences. This significantly speeds up design and branding work.

Conclusions and Recommendations

Using AI in design helps save time, reduce costs, and speed up time-to-market. Experiment with different tools, combine their capabilities, and create truly unique products.
